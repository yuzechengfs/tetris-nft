# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026

Tetris NFT respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data.

---

## 📊 Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the app:

- Screenshots created by the user (for NFT minting)
- Wallet address (for blockchain interactions)
- Basic app usage data (for functionality only)

We do NOT collect:
- Personal identity information
- Contacts
- Location data
- Background tracking data

---

## 🔗 Blockchain Data

When minting NFTs:

- Your wallet address is used to mint NFTs on the Solana blockchain
- Blockchain transactions are public and immutable
- We do not control or store blockchain data

---

## 📸 User Content

- Screenshots used for NFTs are created and owned by the user
- The app does not claim ownership over user-generated content

---

## 🔐 Data Storage

- Most data is stored locally on your device
- Blockchain-related data is handled via your wallet

---

## 🛑 Third-Party Services

The app may interact with:

- Solana blockchain
- Wallet providers

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

---

## 👶 Children’s Privacy

This app is not specifically targeted at children under 13.

---

We may update this policy. Updates will be reflected in this document.
